Lahore Travel Guide

Lahore, Pakistan:

Often referred to as the "Heart of Pakistan", Lahore is a vibrant city filled with rich history, colorful bazaars, and a blend of modernity and tradition.

Places to Visit:

  • Badshahi Mosque - a marvel of Mughal architecture
  • Lahore Fort - steeped in centuries of history
  • Shalimar Gardens - peaceful and serene
  • Walled City - take a walk back in time
  • Liberty Market - for the best shopping experience

Things to Do:

  • Enjoy a rickshaw ride through the bustling streets of Anarkali Bazaar.
  • Savor a plate of spicy biryani or nihari at Food Street.
  • Watch the mesmerizing flag-lowering ceremony at Wagah Border.
  • Visit the Lahore Museum to delve into Pakistan's rich history and art.
  • Experience a live Qawwali session at Hazrat Mian Mir's shrine on a Thursday evening.
