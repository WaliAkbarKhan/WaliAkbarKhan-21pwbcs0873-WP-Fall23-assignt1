Day 1: Arrival and Exploring the Old City

After landing at the Allama Iqbal Airport, I hopped onto a rickshaw, and boy oh boy, what a ride that was! Navigated the bustling streets and went straight to the heart of the old city. The Badshahi Mosque took my breath away.

Day 2: A Day for Foodies

Started my day with some mouth-watering halwa puri at the famous street stalls. Then, off to Gawalmandi for some sizzling kebabs in the evening. Yum!

Day 3: Shopping and More

Lahore's bazaars are something else. Anarkali Bazaar was a shopper's paradise. Got some beautiful handicrafts and traditional clothing. Oh, and can't forget the juttis!

Lahore, you've been a blast! Can't wait to visit again.
